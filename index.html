Visit Los Realejos

Where mountains meet Atlantic - the old capital of Taoro Kingdom of Tenerife

Top three things to do at Los Realejos

Spot the jaw-dropping murals.

You can find great pieces of street-art in all neighborhoods (barrios) of Los Realejos

Walk Rambla de Castro - famous cliff path.

Rambla de Castro is an amazing 4 km long path with breathteaking ocean views

Catch a wave or two, on Playa el Socorro

Playa el Socorro is a beautiful black sand beach, very popular with surfers
